Alivio Tourist Park is a WordPress website for a local tourist and caravan park, designed to help visitors easily explore accommodation options, facilities, and park layout before arriving.

Technical Highlights:

  • Built as a custom WordPress theme with extensive use of ACF and custom post types for accommodation and facilities
  • Developed a custom SVG-based park map with toggleable layers to highlight different areas and amenities
  • Implemented a flexible content block system using ACF repeater fields, allowing pages to be assembled from reusable, configurable sections
  • Focused on making content management straightforward for the client, while keeping the underlying structure maintainable and extensible
