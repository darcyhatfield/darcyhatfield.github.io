Spirit Trail is a match-3 battle game built for the Playdate, inspired by Japanese folklore and designed around fast, tactical decision-making. Players match tiles to battle a lineup of 8 yōkai, each with their own unique abilities and attacks. Spirit Trail encourages players to learn enemy behaviours and optimise their team as they uncover the stories of the yōkai they defeat.

My main goal when starting development of Spirit Trail was to push my pixel art skills from what I had done for my previous games. I am really proud of the art I produced for the game and how far my pixel art skills have come from the beginning of the project to the release.
